Prompt: 
Design a responsive, lightweight civic technology website called **AI Legal Rights Navigator**. 

Purpose:
Help citizens understand their legal rights in simple language based on real-life situations. 

Core Features:
1. Situation-to-Rights AI → Users describe problems in natural language, system maps them to relevant laws.
2. Simple Legal Language → Convert complex legal text into easy explanations.
3. Step-by-Step Legal Guidance → Show complaint filing steps, required documents, expected outcomes.
4. Hyper-Local Legal Help → Display nearby police stations, legal aid centers, consumer courts.
5. Multilingual Support → Hindi + regional languages, with voice/SMS input for rural accessibility.
6. Low Bandwidth Accessibility → Text-first interface, minimal data usage, chat-style interaction.

Pages/Sections:
- **Home Page**: Intro banner, “Describe Your Problem” input box, quick categories (Consumer Rights, Worker Rights, Women’s Rights, Tenant Rights, Digital Fraud).
- **How It Works**: Visual flowchart (Problem → AI → Rights → Action Steps → Local Help).
- **Legal Categories**: Expandable cards with simplified law explanations.
- **Nearby Help**: Map integration (OpenStreetMap API) showing local legal aid centers.
- **Languages**: Toggle for Hindi/regional languages, voice input option.
- **About & Impact**: Explain empowerment, access to justice, governance insights.

Design Style:
- Clean, minimal, mobile-first.
- Accessible fonts, high contrast for readability.
- Icons for categories (e.g., scales of justice, shield, handshake).
- Chat-style interface for problem input.

Technology Stack (for developer handoff):
- Frontend: HTML/CSS/JavaScript
- Backend: FastAPI/Flask
- Database: PostgreSQL/SQLite
- AI/NLP: Intent classification models
- Mapping: OpenStreetMap API
- Voice: Speech-to-text integration
- Hosting: Cloud free-tier deployment

Output:
Generate a modern, user-friendly website layout with clear navigation, interactive chat input, and localized legal help integration. Ensure accessibility for low-bandwidth rural users.
